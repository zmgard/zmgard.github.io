Econometric Panel Data Analysis – Wage Determinants

Role: Data Analyst / Econometrics Lab

A class project at the University of Oklahoma where we were tasked with selecting a dataset and conducting a study to determine what factors affect individual wages over time. I used R and libraries including tidyverse, plm, and estimatr to clean the data, calculate within-person variance, and run Pooled OLS, Fixed Effects, and Random Effects regression models.

  • Explored within-person variance for education, marital status, union membership, and rural residence
  • Compared fixed effects, random effects, and pooled OLS estimates to analyze panel data
  • Interpreted coefficients to understand wage impacts of union membership and other demographic factors
  • Produced clear, reproducible results suitable for academic reporting
